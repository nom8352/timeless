# Kids Cake Smash Photography Brisbane

Celebrate your child's first birthday with a fun and memorable Cake Smash session!

## What to Expect
- **The Setup**: We work with you to choose a theme or color scheme that suits your style.
- **The Smash**: Let your little one explore their first birthday cake while we capture the mess and the joy.
- **The Splash**: Often followed by a "bubble bath" in a tiny tub to clean up and get some extra cute photos.

It's a delightful way to mark the end of the first year and the beginning of toddlerhood.
